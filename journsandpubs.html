Medical Writing

Medical Writing is a publication that is produced quarterly by the European Medical Writers Association. It's goal is to educate and inform medical writers in Europe, but also the entire world. Each issue has a specific theme along with topics that are relevant to the practice of medical writing. Articles can provide:

  • Practical advice to medical writers
  • Guidelines and reviews/summaries/updates of guidelines published elsewhere
  • Original research
  • Opinion pieces
  • Interviews
  • Review articles

    • American Medical Writers Association (AMWA) Journal

    The American Medical Writers Association (AMWA) Journal is a national, peer-reviewed publication that is compiled and issued by the American Medical Writers Association. This journal contains information about the "knowledge, skills, and opportunities in the field of medical communication". A subscription of this journal is included when one becomes a member to the AMWA.
